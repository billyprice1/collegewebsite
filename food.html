Top 3 Reasons to visit Weymouth

Admiral Hardy

Admiral Hardys is one of the best places places to grab a bite to eat the prices for the food are really good and the service is really quick it is located near portland

The Little Ship

The Little Ship is located on the enterance of the Isle of Portland they always have something good to offer from there wide menu selections they serve food all day from 12pm in the summer it an good place to visit as there are beach surronding with seating area

Fish 'n' Fritz

Fish 'n' Fritz is the best chip shop in Weymouth Fish ‘n’ Fritz has been awarded regional winner and national runner – up for UK’s Fish and Chip of the year competition.
